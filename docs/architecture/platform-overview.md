# Platform Overview

ForgeInsights AI is a decision intelligence platform that unifies company information from APIs, uploaded documents, local files, and internal systems.

## Operating modes

### Silent intelligence
The platform continuously ingests and analyzes data to detect failures, workflow inefficiencies, business risks, and strategic opportunities.

### Conversational intelligence
Users can ask questions in chat and receive grounded answers based on company data, documents, and connected systems.

## Product outcomes

- Better decision-making
- Faster problem detection
- Improved operational workflows
- Stronger visibility across teams and tools
- Enterprise knowledge access through chat
